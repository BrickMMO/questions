# The Bridge mRiddle

## The Setup

Four people (A, B, C, and D) must cross a narrow bridge at night as fast as possible. How fast can the cross the bridge?

- **Capacity:** The bridge only holds **two people** at once.
- **The Torch:** One torch is required. It must be carried back and forth.
- **The Speeds:** A (1 min), B (2 mins), C (5 mins), D (10 mins).
- **The Rule:** A pair moves at the speed of the **slower person**.

### Step-by-Step Walkthrough:

1. **A & B** cross together — _2 mins._ Total: **2 mins**
2. **A** returns with the torch — _1 min._ Total: **3 mins**
3. **C & D** cross together — _10 mins._ Total: **13 mins**
4. **B** returns with the torch — _2 mins._ Total: **15 mins**
5. **A & B** cross together again — _2 mins._ Total: **17 mins!**

**The Trick:** You must send the two slowest people (C & D) together so their times overlap, but neither of them should be the one to bring the torch back!
