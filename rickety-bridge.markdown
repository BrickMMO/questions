# The Bridge mRiddle

## The Setup

Four people (A, B, C, and D) must cross a narrow bridge at night as fast as possible. How fast can the cross the bridge?

- **Capacity:** The bridge only holds **two people** at once.
- **The Torch:** The group has one flashlight. It is needed to cross the bridge.
- **The Speeds:** Each member in the group requires a different amount of time to cross the bridge. Person A requires 1 minute, B requires 2 minuntes, C requires 5 minutes, and D requires 10 minuntes.
- **The Rule:** A pair moves at the speed of the **slower person**.

### Solution: 17 Minutes

## Step-by-Step Walkthrough:

1. **A & B** cross together — _2 minutes._ Total: **2 minutes**
2. **A** returns with the torch — _1 minute._ Total: **3 minutes**
3. **C & D** cross together — _10 minutes._ Total: **13 minutes**
4. **B** returns with the torch — _2 minutes._ Total: **15 minutes**
5. **A & B** cross together again — _2 minutes._ Total: **17 minutes!**

**The Trick:** You must send the two slowest people (C & D) together so their times overlap, but neither of them should be the one to bring the torch back!
